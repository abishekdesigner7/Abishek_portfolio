Case Study

Mayuri Residency

Designing a Premium Hotel Booking Experience

Role UI/UX Designer
Timeline 1 Week
Tools Figma, Claude AI
Type Hotel Website
Overview

Project Overview

Mayuri Residency is a premium hotel located in the heart of Bengaluru's Electronic City. They needed a modern, trustworthy website that could serve as their primary booking platform and digital presence.

The goal was to design a website that reflects the hotel's warm hospitality while making the room booking process seamless and intuitive for guests.

Key Objectives

  • Create a premium, trust-building visual identity online
  • Design an intuitive room booking flow
  • Showcase amenities and location effectively
  • Ensure responsive experience across all devices
  • Drive direct bookings through the website
The Challenge

Problem Statement

01

No Online Presence

The hotel lacked a professional website, relying solely on third-party booking platforms with high commission rates and limited branding control.

02

Trust Gap

Without a dedicated website, potential guests had no way to verify the hotel's quality, amenities, and reviews before booking — leading to lost conversions.

03

Booking Friction

Guests had to call or use third-party apps to book rooms, creating unnecessary friction in the reservation process and reducing direct bookings.

Research

Research & Discovery

Competitive Analysis

I analyzed 5+ hotel websites across Bengaluru to understand common patterns in hotel web design. Key findings included:

  • Hero imagery is crucial — high-quality property photos immediately build trust
  • Booking CTAs need to be visible above the fold without feeling pushy
  • Reviews and ratings significantly impact booking decisions
  • Location accessibility is one of the top decision factors for business travelers

Target Audience

Primary User

Business Traveler

Age 28-45, visiting Bengaluru for work. Values convenient location, reliable Wi-Fi, clean rooms, and quick check-in. Books hotels within 24-48 hours of travel.

Secondary User

Family Traveler

Age 30-50, planning a Bengaluru trip with family. Looks for spacious rooms, safety, nearby attractions, and value for money. Books 1-2 weeks in advance.

Process

Design Process

01

Empathize

Understood the hotel's goals and guest expectations through discussions with the stakeholder. Identified that trust and ease of booking were the primary concerns.

02

Define

Mapped out the information architecture: Home, Rooms, Amenities, Gallery, Location, Reviews, and Contact. Prioritized booking CTA placement and visual storytelling.

03

Ideate

Explored multiple layout concepts — from full-screen immersive imagery to card-based room displays. Settled on a hero-driven approach with floating elements for a modern feel.

04

Design

Created high-fidelity mockups in Figma with a dark, elegant color palette. Designed room cards with pricing, image galleries with lightbox, and a multi-step booking form.

05

Build & Test

Developed the website with responsive design, tested across devices, and iterated on the booking flow based on usability feedback. Deployed on Render for production hosting.

Design Decisions

Key Design Decisions

Dark, Elegant Theme

Chose a dark color scheme with warm gold accents to convey luxury and premium hospitality. This creates an immersive, sophisticated feel that matches a high-end hotel brand.

Prominent Booking CTA

Placed "Book Your Stay" and "Explore Rooms" CTAs above the fold in the hero section. The booking action is always accessible without scrolling, reducing drop-offs.

Social Proof Integration

Added star ratings (4.4/5), review count, and guest testimonials prominently. Social proof near the booking CTA significantly increases conversion confidence.

Mobile-First Responsive

Designed mobile layouts first, ensuring the booking flow works perfectly on phones. Over 60% of hotel bookings come from mobile devices, making this critical.

Visual Storytelling

Used large, high-quality property images in a gallery format to let guests "experience" the hotel virtually. The hero image creates an instant emotional connection.

Clear Navigation Structure

Organized content into logical sections (Rooms, Amenities, Gallery, Location) with a sticky header for easy access. Guests find what they need within 2 clicks.

Outcome

Results & Impact

100%

Responsive across all device sizes — desktop, tablet, and mobile

6+

Key pages designed including Home, Rooms, Gallery, Location, Reviews, and Contact

2-Click

Booking flow — guests can initiate a reservation within 2 clicks from any page

Key Learnings

  • Trust is everything in hospitality: High-quality imagery and social proof are not optional — they directly impact booking decisions.
  • Simplicity wins: The booking flow should have minimal steps. Every additional field or page increases abandonment rates.
  • Mobile matters most: Designing for mobile first ensured the experience was great where most users actually browse hotel sites.
  • AI accelerates design: Using Claude AI for rapid prototyping and code generation cut the development timeline significantly.
